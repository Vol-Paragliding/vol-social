Welcome to Vol

Vol is a platform for paragliding enthusiasts to share their flights and connect with others in the community.

About Vol

Vol is designed to bring paragliders together, allowing them to share their flying experiences, track their flights, and stay connected with the community. Our platform offers detailed flight logs, interactive maps, and a vibrant community forum.

Features

  • Flight tracking and logging
  • Interactive maps to view flight paths
  • Community forum to connect with other paragliders
  • Personal profile to showcase your flying achievements
  • Secure and private data handling

Data Privacy

We prioritize user privacy and data security. Please read our privacy policy to understand how we handle your data. Our app only collects essential information to provide and improve our services.
